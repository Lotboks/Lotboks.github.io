About Us


Aqua dance club was established in 1999 in Skopje, Macedonia, and has been successfully ran till this day.
It's one of the biggest and oldest dance schools for hip hop and contemporary dance in Macedonia, on top of being one of the most awarded dance schools.
Aqua dance club is a co-founder of the Macedonian Association of Modern Dances since 2011. Part of the International Dance Organization - IDO since 2012.
During it's 20 years of work, Aqua has had many projects done.
From original choreographies for TV shows, Festivals and Sport Events , to competition numbers with which Aqua regularly participate in dance competitions in both Macedonia and Internationally.
Here are some of the most memorable ones:

Choreographies for Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2005 and 2015
TV Dance show "Between school desks" - 2002
Dance performance "Dance Your Life Off" - 2019
Represents Macedonia on an International level successfully since 2010
Vice-champions junior hip hop formation Loret Del Mar, Spain 2010
Three time finalists on World Ido Hip hop Championships: 2012 - Koper, Slovenia ; 2015 - Rimini, Italy and 2017 - Copenhagen, Danmark
Champions of junior hip hop division, the best junior choreography and the Best Choreography overall - 2016 - Rome, Italy
Winners of many international competitions and festivals in various dance disciplines and categories.
As a last noteworthy mention, starting from 1999 Aqua to this day is a state champion in various dance disciplines and categories.
